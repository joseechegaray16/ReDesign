CT's Hottest Store

The Angry Pepper is a great little family-run business carrying all things hot and spicy! From the cayenne pepper to the Carolina Reaper, we've got a great selection of award-winning hot sauces, BBQ sauces, wing sauces, salsas, spices, condiments and more. We've got hot nuts, hot potato chips, hot popcorn, hot bloody mary mix and more! Not only do we carry hot and spicy products from many of the best known, but we also carry products made by some of Connecticut's very own, including , Willy Petes Chocolates, Hot Lady, Sam and Oliver, Holy Hannah , Hot Monkey nuts, Tomspice and more!
